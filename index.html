Thesis Electronic Eng:
  Deep Learning for Sign Language Recognition

I developed a simple DL model toolkit DeeLeMMa from scratch to see the insights of the process. I used DeeLeMMa to train a CNN for American Sign Language (ASL) classification task. I compared my results to another model with transfer learning and combined it with detection to build a real-time demo.

Thesis Physics:
  Cosmological N-body simulations

I analyzed the methods and results used in the Millennium simulation, a cosmological N-body simulation of the evolution of the matter distribution in the universe.
